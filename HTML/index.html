Portugal

Portugal is a country located in southwestern Europe on the Iberian Peninsula. It is known for its rich history, beautiful coastline along the Atlantic Ocean, and cultural heritage. The capital city is Lisbon, and the official language is Portuguese. Portugal has a diverse landscape that includes beaches, mountains, and picturesque villages. The country has played a significant role in global exploration and has a strong maritime tradition. It's also renowned for its delicious cuisine, including dishes like bacalhau (salted codfish) and pastéis de nata (custard tarts).

France

France is a country located in Western Europe known for its rich history, diverse culture, and iconic landmarks. The capital city is Paris, famed for the Eiffel Tower and art museums like the Louvre. France has a varied landscape that includes mountains, rolling countryside, and beautiful coastlines along the Mediterranean Sea and the Atlantic Ocean. The country has been a major cultural and artistic center, influencing fashion, cuisine, and philosophy. With its historical significance and modern contributions, France remains a prominent global destination.

Spain

Spain is a country in southwestern Europe known for its vibrant culture, diverse landscapes, and historical heritage. The capital is Madrid, and Spanish is the official language. Spain offers a mix of modern cities and traditional villages, from the bustling streets of Barcelona to the historic charm of Seville. It boasts beautiful Mediterranean beaches, rugged mountains like the Pyrenees, and arid plains. Spain's cultural influence includes flamenco music, bullfighting, and a rich culinary scene featuring dishes like paella and tapas. Its history includes Moorish architecture, exploration, and artistic contributions from figures like Picasso and Gaudí.

CAPITALS

Lisbon is the capital city of Portugal, characterized by its captivating blend of historic charm and modern energy. It's famed for its colorful neighborhoods, scenic viewpoints, and proximity to the Atlantic Ocean. Lisbon showcases a unique cultural heritage, warm climate, and iconic landmarks like Belém Tower and São Jorge Castle. With its rich history and coastal allure, Lisbon offers an inviting atmosphere for visitors to explore.

Paris is the capital of France, renowned for its iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum. It's a center of art, culture, and fashion, with a rich history and romantic ambiance that draw visitors from around the world.

Madrid is the capital city of Spain, known for its lively culture, historic architecture, and vibrant arts scene. It's home to famous museums like the Prado Museum and landmarks like the Royal Palace. Madrid offers a blend of traditional Spanish charm and modern urban energy, making it a captivating destination for travelers.
